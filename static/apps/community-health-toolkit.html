Community Health Toolkit

Quick, explainable screening calculators for outreach, clinics, and health fairs.

Offline-ready · No data leaves this device

Body Mass Index

A quick screening number for weight status. Not valid for children, pregnant women, or highly muscular adults — say so if it applies.

Enter weight and height to see the result.

Blood Pressure Category

Based on American Heart Association adult categories. A single reading is a snapshot, not a diagnosis — encourage repeat checks.

Enter both numbers to see the category.

Daily Hydration Estimate

A general planning estimate for fluid needs — not medical guidance for people with kidney, heart, or fluid-restriction conditions.

Fill in the fields to see a daily estimate.

Type 2 Diabetes Risk Screener

Modeled loosely on the CDC Prediabetes Risk Test factors. A screening prompt only — a high score means "get tested," not "you have diabetes."

Answer the questions to see a risk indication.

FOR EDUCATION & SCREENING AWARENESS ONLY. These tools do not diagnose any condition and do not replace clinical evaluation. Refer anyone with concerning results, or anyone in visible distress, to a qualified health worker or clinic promptly.
